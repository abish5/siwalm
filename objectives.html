Objectives

SIWALM's objectives are to:

  • Train undergraduate students, young professionals and policy makers in integrated water management principles and practices, efficient agriculture water management strategies and modern irrigation technologies through workshops, short-term and long-term courses
  • Train farmers and extension workers in sustainable agriculture practices, improved irrigation technologies and enterprise skills through farmer field schools
  • Carry out basic and applied research for developing strategies for efficient management of on-farm water resources to enhance agricultural productivity and ecosystems sustainability
  • Promote and implement Integrated River Basin Management (IRBM) approach in the river basins of Somalia
  • Provide consultancy, extension and advisory services in irrigation development and water management activities
