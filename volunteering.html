Volunteering

Become a Volunteer for Sustainable Development - Share your skills and transform lives.

Volunteering is at the heart of SIWALM's effort for Sustainable Development. We know that people helping people is the best way to address the development needs of our time. Be part of the Somali diaspora movement that is making a real difference in living standards of Somali rural population through the Millennium Developments Goals, particularly eradication of extreme poverty, empowerment of marginalized communities, environmental sustainability and global partnership for development.

Some ways to volunteer with SIWALM are fund raising for our development programs, project development and management, research, writing and editing, outreach and advocacy, community organization, field work and administration.

Guest Faculties

Eminent scientists, professors and natural resources management (water, land and soil) professionals from abroad and within the country are invited for sharing their field experiences in irrigation development, water resources management (surface water and groundwater), soil and water conservation, spate irrigation, rainwater-harvesting, water supply and sanitation development, agribusiness and micro finance.
